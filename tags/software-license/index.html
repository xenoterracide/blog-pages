I need a new favorite OSI license or Why I don’t like the GPL anymore

Fri Aug 6, 2010 by Caleb Cushing in  ironman, Open Source, osi, software license, gpl

I don’t like the GPL anymore after the WordPress Theme fiasco. I don’t like the idea that a theme, plugin, addon, extension, whatever is ‘derived’ from the thing it’s adding on to and thus must also be GPL. If I build a deck onto the back of my house is that deck derived from my house? No! if I take the blueprints of my house and build another one that’s 80% like it is that house derived from my house?
