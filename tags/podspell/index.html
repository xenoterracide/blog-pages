The ShareDir Problem

Thu Feb 27, 2014 by Caleb Cushing in  Pod::Spell, perl

Some of you may have noticed a while back that converted Pod::Spell to the use of File::ShareDir::ProjectDistDir instead of keeping the wordlist in Pod::Wordlist::DATA. This move was made in conjunction with making Pod::Wordlist an Object, and in preparation for a time when you’ll be able to specify your own wordlist file.
